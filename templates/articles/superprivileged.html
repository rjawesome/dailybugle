Superheroes must recognize their own privilage - October 14, 2021
by Big Red

Yuo know your favorite superheroes...Thor, Iron Man (Tony Stark), Black Panther (T’Challa), the likes. They all have one thing in common; they be privilaged as FUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUUU-- Yeah, you heard me, our superheroes, are PRIVILIGED. Thor, is literally a god, he was born a gad, and is able to help Earth because his peploe are just more powerful than "inferior' humans. Tony Stark is a rich boi, and also very smart.However, he is probably smart only because he was able to afford the best education. T’Challa is the (inherited from his father) king of his people, meaning he’s Black Panther because of his bloodline, not purely based on his skill or intelligence. All of these guys didn’t obtain their role as defender of Earth through a fair competition. Hell, if I was given a top-class education and a ton of money even I could be Iron Man.
