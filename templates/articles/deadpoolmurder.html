Ryan Reynolds Found Dead in New York Home

the tragic death shocked the globe - February 31, 2032
by Ryan Reynolds

Early this morning renowned actor Ryan Reynolds was found dead on his floor in what appears to be an unprovoked homicide. The culprit, the infamous sly mouthed mercenary Wade Wilson, more commonly known as Deadpool, was seen and heard at the scene around the time that it happened.

Residents nearby report hearing loud rustling coming from inside the home and ending with a loud bang. Wade was then quoted saying” Oh My Gosh!! I'm so sorry I didn't mean for it to blow like that”. He was later seen running off the premises with Reynolds' wife, Blake lively laughing in his arms. Was this some part of a large conspiracy hit, or just coincidence.

We at The Daily Bugle have reached out to Lively asking for comment, but have been answered with silence and no response. As of the moment the only clue where they are or would be is Wilson yelling “send for the Spider if you want me!!”
